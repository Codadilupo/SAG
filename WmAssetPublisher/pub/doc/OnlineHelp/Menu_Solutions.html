The Asset Publisher (provided in the WmAssetPublisher package) is an enhancement to the webMethods Integration Server. It is used to extract and publish metadata about user-selected integration assets to the Metadata Library.
To open the Asset Publisher screens, click Asset Publisher under the Solutions menu on the Navigation panel. The Asset Publisher Configuration screen will be displayed by default.
